## Getting Started

Get started with the HypurrQuant DeFi Platform at [app.hypurrquant.com](https://app.hypurrquant.com).

The flow is built around managing your on-chain wealth — **analyze** transparent yield sources, **allocate** capital, then **automate** ongoing management. Throughout, you keep custody: your funds stay in your own wallet via account abstraction, and any automation runs through revocable session keys.

![HypurrQuant DeFi Platform](/images/defi_platform/overview-full.png)

### 1. Connect Your Wallet

Click the **Connect Wallet** button in the top-right corner. HypurrQuant supports multiple wallet connection methods:

- **Privy** — Email or social login with embedded wallet (powered by Account Abstraction)
- **WalletConnect** — Connect any WalletConnect-compatible wallet (MetaMask, Rainbow, etc.)
- **Coinbase Wallet** — Direct Coinbase Wallet integration
- **Telegram** — Login via Telegram OAuth

<Tip>
  HypurrQuant uses **ERC-4337 Account Abstraction**, enabling gasless onboarding. You can start using the platform without holding native gas tokens — and your assets remain self-custodial, held in your own account rather than by HypurrQuant.
</Tip>

### 2. Select a Chain

HypurrQuant currently supports **Hyperliquid (HyperEVM)** as the primary chain. Multi-chain support (Base, Avalanche) is coming soon.

### 3. Explore Pools

Once connected, you'll see the [Pool Explorer](/defi_platform/analyze/pool-explorer) — a list of all available LP pools sorted by TVL. Browse pools across multiple DEXs:

- **PRJX**
- **HyperSwap**
- **KittenSwap**
- **Ramses**

### 4. Mint LP Position

Select a pool, configure your [Price Range](/defi_platform/execute/seamless-minting), deposit tokens, and mint your LP position. Use [Zap Mint](/defi_platform/execute/zap-mint) for single-asset entry.
