## Automation Agent

Let agents manage your positions automatically — within limits you set, and always self-custodial. Automation runs through **revocable session keys** scoped to specific actions, so HypurrQuant can rebalance and re-enter on your behalf but can never withdraw your funds. You can revoke access at any time.

### Planned Features

- **Asset & Position Management**
  - Automated monitoring
  - Smart position adjustments

- **Operation Automation**
  - Auto-rebalancing
  - Re-entry automation
