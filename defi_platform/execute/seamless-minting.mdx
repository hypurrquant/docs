## Seamless Minting

Create LP positions with an intuitive interface. Select a pool from the [Pool Explorer](/defi_platform/analyze/pool-explorer), configure your price range, and mint in one click.

![LP Minting Panel](/images/defi_platform/minting-panel.png)

### LP Minting Panel

When you select a pool, the minting panel appears on the right side showing:

- **Token Pair & DEX** — The selected pool's pair and DEX name
- **Current Price** — Real-time price of the token pair
- **Liquidity Distribution Chart** — Visual representation of existing liquidity across price ranges

### Price Range Selection

Choose how wide your LP position's price range should be:

| Preset | Description | Best For |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| **Narrow** | Tight range around current price | Maximum capital efficiency, higher risk of going out of range |
| **Common** | Balanced range (default) | Good balance of fees and risk |
| **Wide** | Broad price coverage | Lower risk of going out of range, lower capital efficiency |
| **Full** | Full range (0 to infinity) | Similar to traditional AMM, lowest risk |

You can also manually set **Min Price** and **Max Price** using the input fields or +/- buttons for fine-grained control.

### Deposit Amounts

1. **Select token direction** — Use the swap button (e.g., WHYPE ↔ HYPE) to choose deposit direction
2. **Enter amounts** — Input the amount for each token in the pair
3. **Max button** — Click to use your full wallet balance

<Tip>
  Don't want to balance both tokens manually? Enable [Zap Mint](/defi_platform/execute/zap-mint) to deposit a single token and let the platform handle the rest.
</Tip>

### Minting Your Position

1. Select a pool from the Pool Explorer
2. Choose a price range preset or set custom min/max prices
3. Enter deposit amounts
4. Click **Connect Wallet** (if not connected) or **Mint** to create your position

<Warning>
  Ensure you have sufficient token balances before minting. The platform will show your available balance for each token.
</Warning>
