# HypurrQuant DeFi Platform

> Self-custodial wealth management for your on-chain assets — analyze, allocate, automate, all while you keep custody. [Launch App](https://app.hypurrquant.com)

![HypurrQuant DeFi Platform](/images/defi_platform/overview-full.png)

The DeFi Platform is where HypurrQuant's wealth management approach comes to life. You **analyze** transparent on-chain yield sources, **allocate** capital across pools and chains, and **automate** ongoing management — without ever handing over custody. Your funds stay in your own wallet through account abstraction and revocable session keys.

## 1. Analyze — Transparent Yield Sources & Position Analysis

### Pool Explorer
- Browse 30+ LP pools across multiple DEXs on Hyperliquid
- Compare TVL, APR, and fee tiers at a glance

### Position Dashboard
- Account-based LP position status and real-time profit tracking
- Asset inflow route and operation history analysis

### Curation Service
- DEX / Pool / Range optimization recommendations
- DeFi (Lending & Borrow) opportunity curation

---

## 2. Execute — Allocate & Automate (You Keep Custody)

### Seamless Minting
- One-stop bridge + swap + mint with unified UX
- Flexible price range presets (Narrow / Common / Wide / Full)

### Zap Mint
- Auto-swap and deposit all tokens for maximum LP with a single asset
- No need to manually balance token ratios

### Automation Agent
- Automated asset and position management, within limits you set
- Rebalancing and re-entry automation via revocable session keys — never custody

---

## 3. Modular — LP Position Modular Features

### Hedge
- LP position risk hedging

### Collateral Loan
- Loans using LP positions as collateral
