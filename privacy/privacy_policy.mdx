# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 23, 2026

---

## 1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy describes how **HypurrQuant** ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our **self-custodial DeFi terminal** — including our web application at app.hypurrquant.com, our Telegram bot, and related interfaces (collectively, the "Service"). It should be read together with our [Terms of Service](/privacy/terms_of_service) and [Refund Policy](/privacy/refund_policy).

Because the Service is self-custodial, we are designed to collect as little personal data as possible.

---

## 2. WHAT WE DO NOT COLLECT

We **never** collect, request, or store your **private keys, seed phrases, or wallet recovery information**. You retain sole custody of your assets at all times. We cannot move your funds and do not require you to deposit funds with us.

For self-custodial use of the terminal, we do **not** require identity verification (KYC).

---

## 3. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### 3.1 On-Chain and Wallet Data (Public)

- **Public wallet address(es)** you connect to the Service  
- **On-chain activity** associated with your address (balances, positions, transactions, approvals) — this is public blockchain data we read to display your portfolio

### 3.2 Account and Authentication Data

- Where you sign in through an authentication or account-abstraction provider, limited account identifiers (such as an email or social identifier you choose to provide, and a smart-account address)

### 3.3 Telegram Bot Data

- If you use the Telegram bot: your Telegram ID, username, profile name, and the commands or messages you send to the bot

### 3.4 Subscription and Billing Data

- If you purchase a paid plan, billing is handled by a **third-party payment processor**. We do not store your full card or banking details; we receive limited transaction metadata (such as plan, status, and a payment reference) from the processor

### 3.5 Usage and Technical Data

- **Usage data:** features accessed, interactions, and timestamps (often aggregated or pseudonymous)  
- **Technical data:** IP address, device/browser information, session identifiers, and error logs

---

## 4. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

We use information to:

- **Provide the Service:** display your portfolio, build transactions for your signature, and deliver requested features  
- **Operate paid plans:** process subscriptions and manage entitlements  
- **Improve and secure the Service:** analyze usage, fix errors, prevent fraud and abuse, and maintain integrity and availability  
- **Communicate:** send service-related notifications and respond to your requests  
- **Comply:** meet legal and regulatory obligations

---

## 5. LEGAL BASES (WHERE APPLICABLE)

Where data protection law (such as the GDPR) applies, we process personal data on the bases of: performance of a contract (providing the Service), our legitimate interests (security, improvement, fraud prevention), compliance with legal obligations, and your consent where required.

## 6. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with:

### 6.1 Service Providers and Integrations

- **Authentication / account-abstraction providers** (for sign-in and smart accounts)  
- **Payment processor** (for subscription billing)  
- **Analytics and infrastructure providers** (for product analytics, hosting, RPC/network access, and error monitoring)  
- **Trading venues, DEXs, aggregators, and bridges** (such as Hyperliquid, Pacifica, Lighter, and Aster) when you choose to transact

Each third party is subject to its own privacy policy.

### 6.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information to comply with legal obligations or court orders, to protect our rights, property, or safety, to investigate violations of our Terms, or to respond to lawful governmental or regulatory requests.

We implement reasonable, industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit, access controls on a need-to-know basis, and ongoing monitoring. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. This data is captured using first- and third-party cookies and other tracking technologies and is used for site optimization, security, and product improvement. For more information about how Microsoft collects and uses this data, see the [Microsoft Privacy Statement](https://privacy.microsoft.com/privacystatement). By using our sites, you agree to this data collection and use by us and Microsoft.
