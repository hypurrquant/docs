# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 23, 2026

---

## 1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy describes how **HypurrQuant** ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our **self-custodial DeFi terminal** — including our web application at [app.hypurrquant.com](https://app.hypurrquant.com), our Telegram bot, and related interfaces (collectively, the "Service").

Because the Service is self-custodial, much of your activity happens directly on public blockchains through your own wallet. We collect only what we need to operate the Service.

---

## 2. WHAT WE DO NOT COLLECT

- **We never collect or store your private keys, seed phrases, or recovery phrases.**  
- We do not take custody of your funds and do not require deposits to use the self-custodial terminal.  
- For self-custodial use, we do not require identity verification (KYC) to connect a wallet and trade.

---

## 3. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### 3.1 Information You Provide or Connect

- **Wallet address(es)** you connect, and on-chain activity associated with them  
- **Account and authentication data** processed through our authentication / account-abstraction providers (e.g., an email or social identifier you choose to sign in with)  
- **Telegram account information** (Telegram ID, username) if you use the Telegram bot  
- **Subscription and billing data** if you purchase a paid plan — handled by our third-party payment processor (we do not store full card numbers)  
- **Communications** you send to us (support requests, messages, commands)

### 3.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage data**: features accessed, actions taken, and timestamps  
- **Technical data**: IP address, device/browser type, session identifiers, and error logs  
- **Cookies and local storage**: used in the web app for sessions, preferences, and analytics (see Section 9)

### 3.3 Information from Public and Third-Party Sources

- **On-chain data**: balances, positions, and transaction history that are publicly recorded on blockchains for the wallet addresses you connect  
- **Market and venue data**: from trading venues and data providers used to power analytics

---

## 4. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use information to:

- **Provide the Service**: display positions, analytics, routing, and order interfaces, and enable transactions you initiate  
- **Operate subscriptions**: process payments, manage plans, and prevent abuse  
- **Improve and secure the Service**: debug errors, monitor performance, detect fraud, and protect against unauthorized access  
- **Communicate**: send notifications, alerts, and service updates  
- **Comply**: meet legal and regulatory obligations

---

## 5. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

### 5.1 Service Providers

- **Authentication and account-abstraction providers** (for sign-in and smart-account functionality)  
- **Analytics providers** (for product analytics and error tracking)  
- **Blockchain RPC and infrastructure providers** (to read and broadcast on-chain data)  
- **Payment processors** (for subscription billing)  
- **Trading venues and protocols** you choose to interact with  
- **Telegram** (for bot interactions)  
- **Cloud hosting providers** (for secure storage and processing)

These parties process information under their own privacy policies.

### 5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information to comply with legal obligations or court orders; to protect our rights, property, or safety; to investigate violations of our Terms; or to respond to lawful government or regulatory requests.

---

## 6. DATA SECURITY

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit, access controls on a need-to-know basis, and ongoing monitoring. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for the security of your own wallet and credentials.

---

## 7. DATA RETENTION

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide the Service, comply with legal and regulatory obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Note that data recorded on public blockchains is outside our control and cannot be altered or deleted by us.

---

## 8. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your personal information, or to object to certain processing. You may also withdraw consent where processing is based on consent. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details in Section 13. We will respond as required by applicable law.

If you are in the EU/UK, our legal bases for processing include performance of a contract, our legitimate interests (operating and securing the Service), compliance with legal obligations, and your consent where applicable.

---

## 9. COOKIES AND TRACKING

The web application uses cookies, local storage, and similar technologies for session management, preferences, and analytics. The Telegram bot relies on Telegram's platform and session identifiers rather than web cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling them may affect functionality. Analytics data is used in aggregated or pseudonymized form to improve the Service.

## 10. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) or your consent for such transfers.

## 11. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The Service is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will promptly delete it.
