## 4. REFUND ELIGIBILITY

**14-day refund window.** If you are a first-time subscriber, you may request a full refund of your most recent subscription charge within **14 days** of that charge, provided the request is made in good faith.

Refunds are generally **not** available in the following cases:

- The 14-day window for the relevant charge has elapsed.  
- A refund has already been issued for a prior billing period on the same account.  
- The charge relates to a renewal that you had the opportunity to cancel before the renewal date.  
- The account is suspended or terminated for violation of our Terms of Service or for suspected fraud or abuse.

We may decline refund requests that we reasonably determine to be abusive, fraudulent, or repeated.

## 5. NON-REFUNDABLE AMOUNTS

The following amounts are **non-refundable** because they are paid to or executed on third-party networks and venues and cannot be reversed by us:

- **Blockchain network (gas) fees** incurred when you submit transactions.  
- **Exchange, venue, and protocol fees** charged by third parties (e.g., trading venues, DEXs, bridges).  
- **On-chain aggregator swap fees** (such as the 0.05% swap fee that balances token ratios when minting LP), because these are executed on-chain at the time of the transaction and are non-reversible.

These pass-through amounts are distinct from subscription fees and are never refunded by us.
