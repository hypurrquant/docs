## What You’ll Need

To begin, prepare the following:

- A centralized exchange account with USDC (Binance, Coinbase, etc.)
- A crypto wallet (MetaMask, Rabby, etc.)  
  → We recommend **Rabby Wallet** for better compatibility and usability.
