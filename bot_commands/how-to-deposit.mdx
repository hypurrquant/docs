title: "How to Deposit"
---

To begin, prepare the following:

- A crypto wallet (Rabby, Phantom, Backpack, etc.)  

## Deposit with Unit Protocol.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Start HypurrQuant bot" img="/images/onboarding/unit_address.jpeg">
    Bot automatically creates an EVM wallet and issues the corresponding Unit deposit address. 
    Please make sure to deposit at or above the minimum required amount.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Send Asset from Wallet" img="/images/onboarding/send_solana.jpeg">
    Please transfer USDC (on the Arbitrum network) to your newly generated
    wallet address.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Receive Asset" img="/images/onboarding/after_deposit.jpeg">
    After the deposit is confirmed, you can see the asset in your wallet.
    You can also check the transaction history on the Unit Protocol website.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Spot Sell" img="/images/onboarding/sell.jpeg">
  You can sell the deposited asset on the spot market 
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Deposit with Hyperliquid
<CardGroup cols={2}>
